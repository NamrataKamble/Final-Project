OUR STORY

Hungry Panda is the most trusted service not only for Indian tiffins but also breakfast/lunch/dinner throughout the Mumbai! We serve hundreds of families, students, and elderly on a weekly basis providing them mouthwatering meals that are less oily, less salty and include the perfect amount of spices, just like it was made at your home! Our all natural Whole Wheat Tawa Roti's are so tasteful and filling. We don't use chemicals or preservative like you'd find in ready-made frozen foods. Our "Ghar Ki Rasoi" style approach in preparing the food makes us stand out from our competitors. So, if you're pressed for time, looking for a fresh, healthy, and homestyle Indian food option delivered right to your doorstep, then you're at the right place!
